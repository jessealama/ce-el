This paragraph includes some more complex dashes that should be en-dashes. In particular, some ranges: i-iii, I-IV, and XLV-LVI. Other ranges are mixed, such as 234a-235b. Finally, some ranges use parentheses, such as (1)-(4), (i)-(v), and even letter-denoted ranges such as (a)-(d). These should all use en-dashes.
